SURYA

Saravanan Sivakumar (born 23 July 1975), known by his stage name Suriya, is an Indian actor and film producer. He primarily works in Tamil cinema[4][5] where he is one of the highest paid actors.[6][7] Considered one of the finest actors of Indian cinema,[5] Suriya has received numerous accolades including two National Film Awards,[8] six Filmfare Awards South and five Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.[9] He has featured six times in the Celebrity 100 list of Forbes India, which takes into account the earnings of Indian celebrities.[10][11]
